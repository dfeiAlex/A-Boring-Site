How To Play

Play a game of rock paper scissors with the computer! Each hand beats, loses to or ties with another. The first to 5 wins!


Rules:

  • To win the round, you must role a hand that beats the opponents hand.
  • You lose if the opponent roles a hand that beats yours.
  • A tie happens when both players role the same hand
